Our Work
to fulfill a child's dream to go to college

What we are doing

We help poor talents of Nepal to pursue their college dream.

Free Scholarship

We evaluate students economic condition. Once eligible, students choose the college to study, they choose their desire subject. We pay their entire fee. They don't have to pay single penny until they finish their education.

Mentorship Program

We do not just pay their college fee, we mentor them, we coach them until they graduate.

Sponsor, School, Student(3S Model)

We work on 3S model.
(S)We find sponsor/donor for student.
(S) We co-ordinate with schools and colleges for admission and fee payment.
(S) We consult students and motivate them to complete their education.
