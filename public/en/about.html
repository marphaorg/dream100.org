About Dream100.Org

We provide financial assistantship to needy students of Nepal to pursue their dreams of higher education

Dream100 started with the sole purpose of helping students in rural Nepal to continue their higher education. It is a registered non-government organization in Nepal. It's social welfare council registration number is 54092.
